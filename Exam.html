FIFA World Cup 2030

The FIFA World Cup 2030 will be held in Morocco, marking the first time country hosts this prestigious tournament. With 48 teams from around the world. Morocco will provide world-class facilities for both players and fans

Host Cities

Casablanca

The largest city in Morocco, known for its modern architecture and vibrant culture.

Marrakech

A city known for its historical significance, vibrant souks, and beautiful gardens.

Rabat

The capital of Morocco, offering a blend of tradition and modernity along the Atlantic coast.

Fez

A city known for its medieval architecture and as a center of Islamic culture.

Tangier

A city located on the Mediterranean coast, known for its unique blend of cultures.

Agadir

Famous for its beaches and warm climate.
